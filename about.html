Welcome

Who we are

Prestige Living Design is an interior design studio dedicated to creating refined, functional spaces for homes, private clients, and commercial environments. We believe great design is where elegance meets purpose—every detail thoughtfully considered to reflect your lifestyle, brand, and vision.

From concept to completion, we blend creativity, craftsmanship, and precision to deliver timeless interiors that feel personal and elevated. Whether designing a warm residential retreat or a sophisticated commercial space, our goal is simple: to transform spaces into experiences defined by comfort, style, and lasting quality.

History

Prestige Living Design was founded on a simple belief: every space deserves to be both beautiful and purposeful. From our first residential projects to today’s mix of luxury homes and commercial interiors, we’ve stayed committed to craftsmanship, client collaboration, and timeless design that stands the test of time.

Why choose us?

We combine a sharp eye for detail with clear communication and on-time delivery. Every project is treated as a partnership, with your lifestyle, brand, or vision at the centre.

From initial concept to final installation, we work with trusted craftspeople and premium materials to ensure interiors that feel personal, refined, and built to last.

What we do

We offer full-service luxury interior design for residential and commercial clients—from space planning and concept development to sourcing, project management, and installation.

  • Residential: homes, penthouses, and private retreats
  • Commercial: offices, hospitality, and branded environments

Our specialities

Our specialities include refined residential interiors, high-end commercial spaces, bespoke furniture and millwork, material and finish curation, and 3D visualization—so you can experience the design before a single piece is installed.
